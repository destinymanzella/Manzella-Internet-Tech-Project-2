Destiny Manzella

About

Destiny Manzella is a Baton Rouge based singer, guitarist, and songwriter that uses her gifts to inspire, excite, and lead people closer to the heart of God. Her encouraging lyrics mixed with her smooth soulful vocals and rhythmic guitar playing create such a unique sound and atmosphere that you can’t help but dance and sing along. Destiny’s original music ranges from fun easy listening songs to powerful emotion filled testimonies that will bring you to tears. She incorporates all styles that influence her including Christian/Gospel, pop, R&B, soul, funk, and blues. Her music is also very influenced by her birth place of New Orleans.

Destiny’s sound won’t be the only thing that keeps you dancing and singing. The way she performs is another gripping part of the experience she creates. She includes loop pedals and other effects to add her own touch to songs and plays solos over them. Destiny engages with her audience for a fun time and can’t help but dance to the music she’s creating.

Since the age of 12, Destiny has performed at many venues, restaurants, and festivals around Louisiana including New Orleans Jazz Fest, Generations Hall, Tipitina’s, and House of Blues. She has also shared the stage with well known performers including Kermit Ruffins and Donald Harrison Jr.. Now at the age of 21, Destiny is continuing her journey studying music at Loyola University in New Orleans. Currently, Destiny continues to perform around Louisiana and is working on new music set to be released in 2023.
